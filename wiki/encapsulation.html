Encapsulation

Encapsulation1 is the concept that bundles together data and the methods that operate on that data. It focuses on hiding data and keeping is safe from exterior manipulation and misuse.

In the human body, there are several main systems (Circulatory, Nervous, Skeletal, etc.) that work in symphony to keep your main body running smoothly. This is a real life example of encapsulation, the different systems are able to report to the brain, but work independently of each other.
